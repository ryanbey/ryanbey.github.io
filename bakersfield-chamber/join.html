Why Join?

At the Bakersfield Chamber our Business is a Better Bakersfield. We advocate on behalf of our members by promoting a pro-business climate. We educate the business community through our Labor Law & HR Forums and Business Roundtables. We communicate in a timely manner about issues that impact us all. We promote our members businesses through our directory, digital advertising and social media. We engage our members through our Bakersfield Young Professionals group, networking events and committee meetings. We lead our community by creating future leaders through our leadership programs.
