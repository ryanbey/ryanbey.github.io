About Us

Our business is a better Bakersfield. We’re a convener of leaders, a catalyst for action, and a champion for our community. Being a member of the Greater Bakersfield Chamber connects you with more than 1,100 members. You’re part of an organization that represents 50-60,000 jobs in the Bakersfield area – considerable clout when advocating for issues that affect the Central Valley. Your business reaps daily benefit from membership with exposure to other businesses of all sizes. You also realize value from interacting with similar and different types of businesses. Members find that they get out of the Chamber what they take from it. And there’s a lot to take. Join us in Building Something Better.
