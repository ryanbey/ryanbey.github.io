Site Purpose

Mist will be a clean and simple weather website to present information in an easy to comsume and elegant way. It will present necessary information in the form of weather stories with useful images. Along with being easy to use, content will be presented in a way that is easy to quickly consume with minimal effort, with room for further expansion and added features.

More specifically, Mist will present weather information for the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas with a storm center and gallery as well. These town weather pages will show current corresponding weather conditions along with relevant articles and content.
